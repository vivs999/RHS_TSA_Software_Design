About Us

Meet Ravenwood High School’s Team #24088-2, a group of students from with a shared passion for programming and a mission to make technology more accessible. Recognizing the growing need for technology in industries like agriculture, we sought out a challenge that would allow us to bridge this gap—leading us to the TSA Software Development event.

What We Do

We are dedicated to providing innovative software solutions to real-world problems. Our team works on various projects that aim to improve efficiency and productivity through technology.
